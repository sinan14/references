How to order Medicines from Medimall?

  1. Open the App

    Our Medimall App is easily available on Play Store and App Store. Click download and open the app to find 100,000 + Inventories.

  2. Search for a Product

    Search for your desired products using the medimall. Choose your product or medicines. You can easily check the availability, Ingredients, and expiry dates.

  3. Click to order

    Cart your Essentials and click to order. If you have carted any prescribed medicines, you will be given an option to upload the prescription. If you don’t have one, yo u can easily consult our doctors at the comfort of your home and get a prescription. Then you can proceed with your order and choose your shipping address.

  4. Choose your Payment

    Once you have chosen the shipping address, you’ll have to select your payment method. For payment, you can either use cards, UPI apps, net banking, or pay on delivery. After you make your payment, we will confirm your order and notify you of the delivery date.

What makes us Unique?

Humans are bound to have succumbed to illness quite often. With the surge of the pandemic, people are terrified to go to hospitals and pharmacies.Our solution caters to the needs of these individuals. The most affected by these issues are people living in remote areas of India. Through medimall, we aim at digitizing and simplifying the way they live. Through our services, people living in remote areas could avail of consultation services from the top doctors in India. Since we offer our app services in regional languages, people can make hassle-free communication.

Serving You With The Best

The abundance and unavailability of medicines and their supply to these hyper-local areas could be sorted out by the implementation of our app. We also offer services relating to fitness and health and offer a virtual gymnasium fulfilling all requirements from the comfort of their homes.

Our dedicated team will deliver the product after assessment through a 3 level quality check, within a stipulated time.

Our Vision

With medimall, we strive to bridge the gap between the scarcity of healthcare services in rural areas and the abundance of healthcare professionals in urban areas. We are working to balance the scales between these two extremes. We hope to offer the future of health and fitness and deliver that promise to their doorsteps.
