FAQ
How long does it take to receive the referral amount?

As soon as a friend, family member, or any other signs up using your referral code and makes their first purchase, the amount is instantly transferred to your e-wallet.

Is the referral feature only available for medpride members?

No, it is applicable for all members, regardless if you are a premium member or not

What should I do if my referral amount has not been processed yet?

Worry not my friend. You can always contact our customer care executive to resolve this issue as soon as we can.

How many referrals do I need for a reward?

You'll get a reward for each friend you refer. When they make their first health purchase, your reward will be transferred to your e-wallet.
