Frequently Asked Questions
What is the next step?

Once you submit your application on the medimall website, all you have to do is sit tight while our experts go through your application. Once the verification is all done. Our team will get back to you. And you’ll receive a username and password to log in to the doctors portal. And that’s it

Is this applicable for doctors from all states?

Yes, you can. You can apply for consultations from anywhere in the country as long you have a valid state registration from the state you reside in. If you are interested, then you can submit your application through our medimall portal.

Is there any fee for joining?

No fees are required at the time of joining.

What if I encounter a verification error?

If there is any verification error, our team will contact you and request a resubmission of the application.

How will I receive payments?

Your consultation fee will get credited to your bank account every week.

Are online consultations legal?

Yes, telemedicine is perfectly legal as of March 2020. Registered medical practitioners who have acquired MBBS or any degree above it can conduct online consultation as per telemedicine guidelines published by the Indian Medical Council in association with NITI Ayog.
